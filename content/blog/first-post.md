# The Future of Payments

The landscape of payments is changing rapidly. From blockchain to real-time payments, the industry is seeing unprecedented innovation.

## Key Trends
- **Real-time Payments (RTP):** Instant settlement is becoming the norm.
- **Embedded Finance:** Non-financial platforms are offering financial services.
- **CBDCs:** Central Bank Digital Currencies are being explored globally.
